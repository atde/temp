189Teca




We are a charming garden in Lomas de Tecamachalco, Mexico City. Built in 2015 and featuring modern architecture, 189Teca offers a quite private space for your event in a spleepless city. We can accommodate up to 300 people for any social or private event on weekends as well as buisnes meetings or conference on weekdays.
